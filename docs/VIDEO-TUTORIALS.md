# Video Tutorials Plan — SMNTC v2.0

This document outlines the proposed content strategy for the SMNTC v2.0 video tutorial series.

---

## Series 1: The Semantic Engine (Basics)

### Episode 1: What is SMNTC?
- **Goal:** Introduction to the "Semantic Intent" philosophy.
- **Key Concepts:** Intent over Math, The Triad Pipeline (Tokenizer -> Transformer -> Kernel).
- **Demo:** Swapping a `vibe` from `calm` to `storm` and seeing the physics update instantly.

### Episode 2: The Token Dictionary
- **Goal:** Comprehensive tour of available surface and vibe tokens.
- **Demo:** Real-time exploration of `topographic`, `crystalline`, and `plasma` modes.
- **Reference:** Visualizing the differences between `stable` vs `chaotic` vibes.

### Episode 3: Spring Physics & Interactivity
- **Goal:** Understanding state transitions and user reactivity.
- **Key Concepts:** Damped harmonic oscillators, `magnetic` and `shockwave` interactions.
- **Demo:** Creating a "Magnetic Surface" that tracks the mouse with zero custom math.

---

## Series 2: The Mesh Engine (Advanced Sources)

### Episode 4: Dynamic Typography (TextSource)
- **Goal:** Moving beyond simple planes to 3D text.
- **Source Material:** Based on the "Business Name" architectural patterns.
- **Demo:** Loading an `.otf` font and extruding it into a liquified 3D title.

### Episode 5: Brand Identity (SVGSource)
- **Goal:** Converting logos into animated meshes.
- **Source Material:** Walkthrough of the `examples/logo-animation/index.html` implementation.
- **Demo:** Taking a raw SVG `<path>` and applying a `plasma` displacement.

### Episode 6: Real-world Extraction (ImageSource)
- **Goal:** Using the new CV pipeline to vectorize bitmaps.
- **Demo:** Drag-and-dropping a PNG logo and seeing it transform into a 3D topographic surface.

---

## Series 3: The Motion Compiler (Exporting)

### Episode 7: CSS Fallbacks & Accessibility
- **Goal:** Ensuring your site looks great without WebGL.
- **Key Concepts:** SVG Filter pipelines, `prefers-reduced-motion` handling.
- **Demo:** Generating a pure CSS animation fallback from a SMNTC config.

### Episode 8: High-Quality Video Export
- **Goal:** Exporting SMNTC for social media and film.
- **Key Concepts:** WebCodecs, MP4Box.js, Frame-perfect rendering.
- **Demo:** Running a 10-second export loop to produce a hardware-accelerated MP4.

---

## Series 4: Industry Blueprints

### Episode 9: The "SaaS" Look
- **Goal:** Building a professional B2B landing page hero.
- **Preset:** `saas-professional`.
- **Demo:** Setting up a low-intensity topographic grid.

### Episode 10: The "Luxury" Experience
- **Goal:** Creating high-end cinematic branding.
- **Preset:** `luxury-elegant`.
- **Demo:** Using gold palettes, crystalline surfaces, and depth-of-field blur.

---

## Production Requirements
- **Audio:** High-fidelity voiceover (Professional/Neutral).
- **Visuals:** 4K Screen recording, syntax-highlighted code overlays.
- **Duration:** 3–5 minutes per episode.
